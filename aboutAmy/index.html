Amy-Kate

What's your favorite nickname?

AK or Aims.
Everyone at work calls me 'AK' and my little bro calls me 'Aims'.

Which celebrity is your spirit animal?

Definitely Mindy Kaling. She can come across as obnoxious when she's playing Kelly on the Office, but after I read her book 'Is Everyone Hanging Out Without Me?', I instantly wanted to be best friends with her.

If you had to be on a reality TV show, which would it be?

Definitely not the Bachelor.
Or Naked and Afraid. Though maybe that show is synonymous with the Bachelor...
Maybe Who Wants to be a Millionaire. I think I would dominate.

What is your most embarrassing hobby?

Besides making a site like this for myself?
Probably my shameless people watching. If we're in the same room, I'm probably observing you like a wild animal on safari.

Why are your parents the coolest?

1. They look about 15 years younger than they are.
2. They have an awesome sense of humor. When the whole fam gets together, we pretty much laugh till we cry.
3. They accept me for who I am no matter what. And they tell me how much they love me and are proud of me on the regular.

How would you describe yourself?

Energetic, loyal, tenacious, and I laugh probably a little too much.
